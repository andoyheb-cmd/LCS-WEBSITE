Background

School Profile

Lucban Christian School, Inc. (LCS) is a non-stock, non-profit church mission school of the Reformed Church in the Philippines (RCPI) – Lucban Christian Reformed Church. The school is committed to developing the moral, physical, intellectual, and spiritual life of every child. LCS believes that each child possesses God-given gifts that must be nurtured and trained according to Biblical principles.

Established in 1993 in response to the growing demand for quality and affordable Christian education, LCS initially offered Preschool education. Elementary education was added in 1995, and Junior High School in 2016, made possible through the leadership of Rev. Dong Won Lu and the financial support of The Kyungnam Layman's Association (KLA).

LCS is duly recognized by the Department of Education under Government Recognition Nos. K-18, E-18 s. 2003, and S-007 s. 2019 for Preschool, Elementary, and Junior High School, respectively.

Beginning SY 2024–2025, LCS is officially certified as a PEAC-participating school and recognized as a grantee under the Expanded Government Assistance to Teachers and Students in Private Education (E-GASTPE) through the Education Service Contracting (ESC) Program.
